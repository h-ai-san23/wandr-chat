1 Introduction

Wandr Chat ("we," "us," or "our") is a proximity-based messaging application available on iOS. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the Wandr Chat mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

This policy applies to all users of the App regardless of location. Where applicable, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Apple's App Store privacy requirements.

2.2 Location Data

Wandr Chat's core feature depends on your location. We collect location data as follows:

  • Precision: Approximate location by default. You may enable precise location in Settings.
  • Timing: Only while the App is in the foreground ("While Using the App" permission). We do not use background location tracking.
  • Storage: Your last known approximate coordinates are stored to enable proximity discovery. We never store a continuous location history.
  • Display: Other users only ever see approximate distances (e.g., "120m away" or "0.3 mi away") ‚Äî never your exact GPS coordinates, address, or a map pin.
Invisible Mode: When enabled, your location is not shared with the discovery system and you will not appear in any other user's Nearby list. You can still send and receive messages with contacts you have already accepted.

2.3 Usage & Technical Data

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data (via Apple's crash reporting framework)
  • In-app feature interactions (e.g., tabs visited, settings toggled) ‚Äî used in aggregate, never tied to your identity for advertising
  • IP address (used for fraud prevention and rate limiting only)

2.4 Purchase Information

In-app purchases (such as conversation boosts and subscriptions) are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit 2. We receive only a transaction receipt and your subscription status ‚Äî we never receive, store, or process your payment card details, Apple ID, or billing address.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Proximity Discovery: To show you other users within your chosen radius and allow them to see you.
  • Messaging: To deliver messages between users, manage request/accept flows, and enforce distance-gating on conversations.
  • Account Management: To display your profile, remember your settings, and maintain your account.
  • Safety & Trust: To process reports of abusive content, enforce blocks, and apply spam limits on outgoing requests.
  • Service Improvement: To identify bugs, crashes, and performance issues using anonymized diagnostic data.
  • Subscription Management: To verify your purchase entitlements and unlock paid features.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable law, enforce our Terms of Service, and respond to lawful requests.
We do not use your personal information to serve targeted or behavioral advertising, nor do we sell, rent, or trade your data with advertising networks or data brokers.

4 How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users (By Design)

When you use Wandr Chat, some of your information is visible to nearby users by design. This includes your display name, avatar, bio, status text, and approximate distance. Messages you send are visible to the intended recipient only.

4.2 With Service Providers

We may share data with third-party vendors who assist us in operating the App (e.g., cloud hosting, database infrastructure, push notification delivery). These providers are contractually bound to use your data only as directed by us and may not use it for their own purposes.

4.3 For Safety and Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to: comply with a legal obligation; protect the rights, property, or safety of Wandr Chat, our users, or the public; prevent fraud or abuse.

4.4 Business Transfers

If Wandr Chat is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your personal information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

  • Messages: Stored for as long as the conversation exists. Declining a request or blocking a user removes the associated messages from your view.
  • Location data: Last known approximate location is overwritten on each app session. We do not retain a location history.
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, we permanently delete your profile, messages, and associated data within 30 days. Anonymized aggregated data (e.g., usage statistics) may be retained.

7 Children's Privacy

Wandr Chat is not directed to children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 17. The App is rated 17+ on the App Store. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 17, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you believe we may have collected information from a child under 17, please contact us immediately.

8 Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.
